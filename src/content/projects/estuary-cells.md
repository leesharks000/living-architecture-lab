Floating freshwater cells positioned at river-sea boundaries. Stewardship rather than extraction: the cell cleans water without impounding or hoarding it.

Deployment targets: Bhitarkanika Wetland (India, Ramsar site), African coastlines, Turkish wetlands, South Asian estuaries. A lab-scale flume test exists; deployment awaits funding.
