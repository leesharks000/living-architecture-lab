A macro-ant is a structure with passengers, where the structure has its own goals and the passengers ride along through negotiation rather than command. Queen engineering manages worker behavior through environmental design rather than direct control.

Anamorphic eye surfaces mean the macro-ant *reads as seeing*, not as surveilling.
