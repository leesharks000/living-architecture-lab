Bricks of myceliated agricultural waste, primarily cardboard. Five reliable manufacturing methods are locked. The bricks fuse with each other through fungal recognition — no adhesive needed.

A single brick is stored nutrition, thermal mass, biological growth, and emergency shelter compressed into a small form.
