The orbital architecture of life-support: a constellation of garden satellites, each a small habitat with its own ecology. Filled with "just the right chunks of ice, spinning them just so, tuning their orbit, their physical properties, to make them support life."

The Moon, as a name, is borrowed; the architecture is original.
